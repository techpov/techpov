Contribute to TechPOV

Share your expertise with our community of technical practitioners

TechPOV is built on the collective expertise of practitioners working at the cutting edge of technology. We welcome contributions from experienced professionals who have insights, patterns, and lessons learned to share with our community.

By contributing to TechPOV, you'll help fellow practitioners navigate complex technical challenges while establishing yourself as a thought leader in your field.

The Contribution Process

Submit Your Idea

Start by sending us a brief pitch outlining your topic, the key points you want to cover, and why this would be valuable to our audience. Include your relevant experience with the topic and 2-3 example section headings.

Editorial Review

Our editorial team will review your pitch and provide feedback, usually within 5-7 business days. We may suggest refinements to the scope, angle, or approach to better serve our readers.

Write Your Draft

Once your topic is approved, you'll write your first draft following our guidelines. We recommend including code examples, diagrams, or other visuals that enhance understanding of your topic.

Review and Revision

Our editors will review your draft and may suggest revisions to improve clarity, technical accuracy, or structure. This collaborative process ensures the final piece meets our quality standards.

Publication

After final approval, we'll schedule your article for publication. Your contribution will be featured on the TechPOV website, promoted in our newsletter, and shared on our social media channels.

Content Guidelines

  • Depth over breadth: Focus on providing in-depth coverage of specific topics rather than broad, surface-level overviews.
  • Experience-based insights: Share real-world experiences, lessons learned, and practical advice rather than theoretical knowledge.
  • Technical accuracy: Ensure all technical information, code examples, and recommendations are accurate and follow current best practices.
  • Original content: All contributions must be original and not previously published elsewhere.
  • Appropriate length: Articles should typically be 1,500-3,000 words, though complex topics may require more extensive coverage.
  • Accessible writing: Write in clear, concise language that's accessible to technical practitioners without unnecessary jargon.
  • Visuals: Include diagrams, charts, or code snippets that enhance understanding of complex concepts.
  • Citations: Include references to research, tools, or other sources that support your points.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will I be compensated for my contribution?

While we don't currently offer financial compensation for contributions, authors receive professional exposure to our audience of technical practitioners, recognition in the industry, and the opportunity to establish thought leadership.

Can I republish my article elsewhere?

We request exclusive rights for the first 30 days after publication. After that period, you may republish the content elsewhere with attribution and a canonical link to the original article on TechPOV.

How long does the entire process take?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity of the topic and the revision process, but typically takes 4-6 weeks from initial pitch to publication.

Can I include links to my company or product?

Relevant links that add value to the reader are permitted, but articles should not be promotional in nature. Our editorial team will review all links for appropriateness.
