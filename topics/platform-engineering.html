Platform Engineering

Strategies for creating developer platforms that accelerate delivery and improve operational stability

The Platform Engineering Approach

Platform engineering has emerged as a discipline focused on building and maintaining self-service internal developer platforms that enable engineering organizations to deliver value faster while reducing cognitive load on developers. By providing paved paths and abstractions over complex infrastructure, platform teams help software developers focus on their core mission: building valuable features for users.

Best Practices

Organize Around Capabilities

Structure platform teams around capabilities (CI/CD, security, observability) rather than projects to build deep expertise and ensure consistent service delivery.

Design for Self-Service

Create intuitive, well-documented interfaces that allow developers to provision resources and capabilities without platform team involvement.

Create Golden Paths

Develop pre-approved, well-supported paths for common engineering workflows that make following best practices the default.

Foster Communities of Practice

Build communities around platform capabilities to facilitate knowledge sharing, gather feedback, and drive adoption of platform services.

Measure Platform Effectiveness

Implement metrics that track platform adoption, developer satisfaction, and improvements in delivery velocity and operational stability.

Embed Security Throughout

Integrate security scanning, policy enforcement, and compliance controls into platform capabilities from the beginning.
